Our People

Principal Investigator

Dr. Meenalosini Vimal Cruz

Lab Director

Dr. Cruz directs the Laboratory of Neural and Somatic Computing (LNSC), focusing on translational AI in medicine. Her research explores interpretable deep learning architectures, multimodal emotion recognition, and equitable clinical decision support systems.

She leads multiple high-impact projects bridging neurodevelopmental informatics with integrative oncology, prioritizing frameworks that offer real-world transparency and trust for clinical practitioners.

Co-Principal Investigator

Akshay Bhuvaneswari Ramakrishnan

AI Engineer & Researcher

Akshay specializes in machine learning, data engineering, and medical artificial intelligence applications. He focuses on the architectural design of complex deep learning systems, including the integration of classical signal processing with self-supervised learning.

His recent work drives the technical development of ocular disease recognition frameworks, advanced survival modeling in integrative oncology, and the optimization of training pipelines for deployment in resource-limited clinical environments.

Research Roadmap (2024–2029)

Ocular Disease & Retinal AI

Comprehensive analysis of representation learning for retinal imaging. Development of a hybrid architecture that trains specialist backbones prior to architectural fusion, surpassing current baseline SOTA models.

  • Representation Learning in Retinal Image Signal Analysis
  • Demographic Adaptability and Privacy via Federated Learning & LLMs

Integrative Oncology

Modeling survival and progression based on metabolic biomarker data (CRP, LDH, Ferritin) and integrating stress/QOL data into prognostic AI frameworks using ML-based survival modeling.

  • Cancer Prognostics via Metabolic Biomarkers
  • Psychological & Quality of Life Factor Integration

Neurodevelopmental Informatics

Synthesizing EEG signatures (U-shaped spectral profile in ASD, TBR in ADHD) and multimodal emotion recognition via EEG and facial expressions for affective computing.

  • Neurophysiological EEG Signatures in ASD and ADHD
  • Multimodal Emotion Recognition Pipeline Design

Agricultural & Edge AI

Deploying explainable AI for practical, rapid-result applications in non-clinical domains to maximize commercial and societal benefits.

  • PestXNet: Explainable Multi-Task Framework
  • Quantum Tuned Hybrid CNN-SVMs

    ICMETE 2025
