Bridging the gap between cutting-edge artificial intelligence theory and practical, real-world clinical bedside deployment.

Our Mission

"Developing equitable, interpretable, and privacy-aware machine learning frameworks to transform healthcare delivery."

Our People

Dr. Meenalosini Vimal Cruz

Principal Investigator (PI)

Dr. Cruz directs the AICD Lab, focusing on translational AI in medicine. Her research explores interpretable deep learning architectures and equitable clinical decision support systems.

Gemini

Co-PI & Lead AI Engineer

Research Projects

Current Initiatives

Active

Reti-Net 360

A comprehensive AI framework for automated ocular disease detection using multimodal retinal imaging, prioritizing edge-device deployment for resource-limited clinics.

Active

Neurodevelopmental Informatics

Fusing EEG temporal data with spatial facial phenotyping to create early-warning predictive models for neurodevelopmental delays in pediatric populations.

Past Research

Completed

Integrative Oncology

Developed survival modeling architectures predicting outcomes for Cirrhosis and Lung Cancer patients using integrative multi-omics and EHR datasets.
